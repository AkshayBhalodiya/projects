Understanding your requirements

Provide us with all the information about your project, letting us know all the elements of the plan as appropriate. We would be asking you basic questions about the primary requirements to assess and understand the project. Based on our assessment, we will deliver a ball-park estimate about the entire project along with the need for allocating the required number of resources.

Our final specification document will be aligned with the requirements of the project, including the detailed quote and timeline for project completion. The requirement document will be include anNon-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) too.

Wireframe, design & prototype

Based on the final specifications, we will deliver the project wireframe. This wireframe will provide an insight into the final product and its appearance.

The clients will get an idea about the elements, the navigation, interaction buttons with the app, and more. The wireframe helps in clearing confusion, and any misunderstanding the team has about the client’s vision. After the wireframe is passed, the team can get started on UI/UX design along with the principal graphical elements.

Development

With the specifications laid out and the wireframe approved, the team will get to designing and developing your project. Our experienced and expert team of developers will ensure that the plan is realized to the tee to make the client’s vision a reality.

Our team uses the latest technologies to ensure the best product is delivered on time.

We will also ensure that there is constant communication from the team about the progress every week. We will send development builds for prompt feedback and review.

Testing & production roll-out

It matters to Cerdonis Technologies LLC to deliver projects of the highest quality and performance. Everything that is developed is delivered after extensive testing at each stage before the build is sent to clients for gaining feedback. We ensure that the testing procedures are of the highest standards for ensuring a successful release of the product or app.

After the final deliverable is ready, we upload the app to the app store. We also deliver additional maintenance and support services for the clients that address their needs. Our support plan ensures that the minor bugs or changes are addressed from a specific period after delivery.

FAQ

01

How do you confirm confidentiality?

We will sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) and confidentiality with you prior to starting any work. This will ensure that no third party is involved during the project/development.

02

How much involvement is required from my end for app development?

After conveying your idea/requirements, we will handle the technical/development part. We will be connecting you with a dedicated project manager who will be managing your project and providing you with regular updated.
We recommend you to stay very active throughout the project so that we are all on track. We also encourage you to feel free by suggesting any changes you would like or let us know if there is something you would like to implement, etc.

03

Can you give me a fixed price quote?

Yes, we can provide you with a fixed quote once we have analysed your requirements.
Analysing requirements may take time as we will be asking small and big questions related to your idea/requirements or project. However, we can always provide a ballpark estimate which can slightly change once the entire scope of work is analysed.

04

Do you build apps for both iOS and Android platforms?

Yes, we develop both iOS and android apps using native technologies. We also provide services for cross platform apps using react native technology.

05

Can I make changes to my app after it’s launched?

Yes, we recommend you to launch the simplest possible version of your idea as a “minimum viable product” (MVP) and continue to adapt it over time.
We can always discuss the potential features or changes you have in mind and continue the app development. The journey of an app does not end when we go live, it is very important to keep on updating the app and giving new features to the users so that they keep using the app.

06

How long does it take to develop an app?

The development time of the app relies on the complexity of the application and its functional specification. The development process takes approximately six to eight months from ideation to deployment.
The quickest app development was 37 days. If you have an urgent requirement, we can involve more resources and get the job done for you.

07

What is your project development process?

Each project is having its own specifications and requirements but the majority of them are having similar development steps. It varies based on the type of project and its specifications.

  • Requirement gathering
  • Analysis
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • UI/UX Designs
  • Development
  • Testing
  • Deployment

08

Which Cloud-based service do you prefer?

The client has to make a choice for a cloud service provider as per their own preference. Examples of cloud services: Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform.

09

How do you ensure the quality of the projects?

Our development team is dedicated to building quality products. We execute formal procedures like periodic code review and design walk-throughs to maintain the quality of the project.
Each project goes through the quality assurance professionals where quality is ensured before the project is delivered to the client.

10

How can I get the status of my project?

A dedicated project manager will be assigned to your project who will take care of your project execution and will update you about the progress of your project. Our PM will be available on skype, WhatsApp, email and calls also. S(he) will ensure that you are always updated with the progress of the project.

11

Do you provide any kind of assistance after project completion?

Yes, once the project is delivered to you, we provide support for bug fixes with no additional charges for 90 days. After the free support period, you can always have the choice to go with paid maintenance and support based on the project’s need.

12

Who will own the proprietary rights of the project?

The project will be owned by the client. After completing the project and payments, all the project assets will be transferred to the client. This includes, project code, designs, documents, etc.

13

How long is the approval process for the app once it’s submitted?

The app approval process takes the maximum time of 1- 2 weeks but it also depends on the complexity of the app. Apple makes its effort to finish the review within a week. In case your app is rejected, we will resolve the issues that apple provides and ask them to review the newer version.
On the other hand, the Google Play store does not a lot of time for the review process of the app.

14

Do I need to test my application?

Yes, you can test the app anytime and provide us with feedback.

15

What about online app builder?

There is a number of online app builder, which provides extremely limited functionality and may be viable in some cases where the requirements/functionality is very limited.
Native development will support any requirement and allow you to go very deep with customisation whereas the online app builder will limit you to very basic functionality. We always encourage to choose native development as that is the best pathway to app development.

16

Can I make changes to my app after it's been launched?

Yes, we highly suggest that you initiate your project by launching the simplest version of your app and continue to make changes over time.
The idea behind upload an app with simple version is that you’ve made certain assumptions, and you can’t test these assumptions until you release the app and based on that uploaded app you can use this information to decide on the priorities of future requirements.
